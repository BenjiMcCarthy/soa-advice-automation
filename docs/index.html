SOA Automation Platform

Intelligent Statement of Advice Generation for Insurance Brokerages

Proposal — March 2026
The Problem

SOA production is your biggest admin bottleneck

Your team spends hours writing Statements of Advice — pulling data from Trail, researching products, drafting recommendations, formatting documents, and checking compliance.

We're building a system that connects directly to your Trail CRM, understands your client's situation, and produces a complete, compliance-ready SOA draft in minutes — not hours. Your advisers review, refine, and approve. That's it.

Compliance

What's in every SOA

Every generated document includes all FMA-required sections, professionally formatted with your company branding.

SectionDescription
Scope of AdviceWhat the client asked for and what advice this document covers
Client ObjectivesGoals, priorities, and concerns in the client's own words
Current SituationIncome, assets, liabilities, dependents, existing cover — pulled from Trail
Needs AnalysisCalculated cover requirements: life, trauma, TPD, income protection, medical
Gap AnalysisCurrent cover vs recommended cover — clear visual comparison
RecommendationsSpecific products recommended with clear reasoning for each choice
Alternatives ConsideredProducts assessed but not recommended, and why
Replacement AnalysisIf replacing existing cover — comparison, risks, and justification
Risks & LimitationsExclusions, stand-down periods, and limitations
Fee & CommissionFull transparency on adviser remuneration
Adviser DisclosureLicence details, complaints process, regulatory information
