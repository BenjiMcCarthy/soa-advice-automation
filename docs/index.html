SOA Automation

AI-generated Statements of Advice in under 60 seconds

The Idea

Replace the SOA bottleneck with AI

Right now you finish a client call, send Tama a voice note, and wait hours or days for the SOA. The client's waiting, the application's stuck, and deals go cold.

The fix: AI takes your voice note (or call transcript), pulls the client data from Trail and comparisons from Quote Monster, and generates a complete branded SOA in under 60 seconds. You do a quick review and send.
